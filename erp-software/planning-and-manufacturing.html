Maximize Efficiency with Streamlined Manufacturing & Planning.

Planning & Manufacturing

Always be prepared to cater to any demand by equipping your manufacturing workflow with the Roadmap ERP system's Planning and Manufacturing suite. Attain cost-efficiency through the Material Requirements Plan (MRP) guided smart production processes, using just the required amount of resources, yet without compromising on productivity, quality, and determined lead-time. The uninterrupted scalability of the suite integrates the activity of Purchasing, Sales and Distribution, Inventory, Customer Relation Management, and the Finance quotients, and streamlines and supports your Planning, Manufacturing, Inspection, Rework and Maintenance activities for either Repetitive or Discrete manufacturing; while adapting to different manufacturing vertical modes, like Assemble-to-Sale order & Make-to-stock.

Benefits of Planning & Manufacturing


Prioritized Manufacturing

Smart prioritization of production based on critical ratio rule, earliest due date, and slack time remaining rule provides optimum customer satisfaction.


Accurate Projections

MRP II's RCCP and CRP run on thoroughly analyzed real-time data, furnishes clear cut solution to your actual resources Vs required resources queries.


WIP Status on a Single Screen

Entire Work in Progress at shop floor is presented on a single screen with representation of work done/ not done status through binary number (0 and 1)
